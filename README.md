# Shalom ✝️

**Supporting peace, healing, and compassionate care.**

Shalom is a health tracking application designed to help caregivers and loved ones monitor seizure episodes and related symptoms. The app makes it easy to record episode duration, periods of nonverbal communication, mobility limitations, recovery progress, and important observations.

The name **Shalom** comes from the Hebrew word for peace, wholeness, and well-being. Inspired by biblical principles of care and compassion, Shalom was created to help families and caregivers keep accurate records during difficult moments while supporting the journey toward healing and recovery.

## Mission

To provide caregivers with a simple and reliable way to track health events, recognize patterns, and share meaningful information with healthcare providers, all while reflecting the biblical values of peace, care, and stewardship.
